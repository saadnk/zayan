About Us

Established in 2019, Zayan Mandi has become a destination for Mandi lovers across Kerala. Nestled in the heart of Thodupuzha and Kayamkulam, we offer a fusion of authentic Arabian flavors and a rich Indian dining experience.

From humble beginnings to becoming a community favorite, our journey is one of dedication, flavor, and culture. At Zayan Mandi, every dish is crafted with passion, using premium spices and traditional cooking techniques to give you the taste of royalty.

Whether you're joining us for a feast with family, a casual lunch, or a celebration, you'll find our space welcoming, our service warm, and our food unforgettable.

At Zayan Mandi, we take pride in offering authentic Arabian recipes made with hygienic and premium ingredients. Our friendly ambience and great service make every visit special. Whether you’re dining with family, hosting an event, or opting for catering, we ensure an experience to remember. Conveniently located in the heart of Thodupuzha and Kayamkulam, Zayan Mandi is your destination for delicious and memorable Mandi.

Our Vision

  • To be the most loved Arabian cuisine destination in Kerala, where every meal tells a story and every guest feels like royalty.
  • To bring the authentic taste of traditional Mandi to every corner of Kerala.
  • To grow into a trusted Mandi brand known for quality, consistency, and care.

Our Mission

  • Serve tasty, high-quality food at a fair price.
  • Always listen and improve based on customer needs.
  • Give back to the community through good causes.
  • Support our team’s growth and well-being.
  • Grow our business sustainably and responsibly.
  • Build strong, long-term partnerships.
